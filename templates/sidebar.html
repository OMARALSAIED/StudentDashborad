    What is a IPS system?

    An IPS (Indoor Positioning System) is a technology that enables the tracking and locating of objects or people within indoor environments.

    Bluetooth-Based Positioning

    Bluetooth beacons are placed at known locations within a facility, and mobile devices equipped with Bluetooth capability detect and measure the strength of signals from these beacons to determine their proximity and location.
